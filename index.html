Image Cropper Guide

Overview

This image cropper tool allows you to select a specific area from an image and download the cropped version. The cropped image maintains a 4:6 aspect ratio.

How to Use

  1. Upload an image: Click the "Choose File" button to upload an image from your device. Once uploaded, the image will be displayed on the canvas.
  2. Select an area: Click and drag the red rectangle to move it over the desired area. Resize the rectangle by clicking and dragging its bottom-right corner. The selected area will maintain a 4:6 aspect ratio.
  3. Preview the cropped image: The preview canvas shows the cropped image in real-time, centered and resized to fit within the preview area.
  4. Download the cropped image: Click the "Download" button to download the cropped image. The downloaded image will have a higher resolution than the preview.

Additional Features

  • Zoom in/out: Use the mouse wheel or pinch gesture on mobile devices to zoom in and out within the red rectangle.
  • Magnet effect: The red rectangle will snap to the edges of the canvas when it's close enough, making it easier to align with the image boundaries.
